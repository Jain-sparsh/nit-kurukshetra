NIT Kurukshetra witnessed an upsurge in its placements during the 2020 recruitment drive as companies brought forth over 573 job offers under different profiles. 538 students from both the UG and PG programs got placed under different sectors of the industry.
Some of the top recruiters who visited the campus were- Amazon, Honda, Nestle, Bharat Petroleum, Airtel, Pepsi, Paytm, Microsoft, Hero, Coca Cola, Goldman Sachs, Indian Oil, Flipkart, and etc.

NIT Kurukshetra Placement Cell

NIT Kurukshetra has placement cell that is single point of contact between the students and companies. The placement team consists of Professor Incharge, Faculty In-charge, Training and Placement officer, Student coordinators and the secretariat. Following steps are taken for the placement of the students:
  • Pre-Placement Talks
  • Conducting Tests
  • Interviews
